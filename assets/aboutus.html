About Us

We are an authentic Mexican restaurant located in multiple states across the U.S. We specialize in Chinese fusion tacos and burritos.

Locations everywhere

We have 7 locations around the U.S. including San Francisco, Sunnyvale, San Jose, Los Angeles, Austin, New York, and West Lafayette.

Always available

Here at N.A.R. we have customer service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Customizable

All of our items can be completely customized. We have tacos, burritos, enchiladas, salads, and fajitas.

Organic

All of our meat is completely organic and all of the animals are raised in non GMO farms.

Fast

We deliver 24/7 and you will recieve your food in under an hour. If not you will get all of your money back.

Online Reservations

You can make a reservation on our website anytime to get a table for parties under 15.
